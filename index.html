Simulation of Startup Reality

FORGE 1.0 isn't just a hackathon. It's a Product Buildathon designed to simulate real-world startup chaos. Teams act as founding engineers, tackling operational bottlenecks with functional solutions.

The focus is on Hardware-Software Integration and creating functional prototypes. We prioritize execution over presentations. Your code must work, your automation must trigger, and your product must solve a real problem.
